Shipping Policy


Thank you for visiting and shopping at Blue Monarch Creations. Blue Monarch Creations orders are shipped directly from our Print on Demand printing partner via their preferred courier services.
You will receive an email update with your tracking number once your order has shipped.

Processing and Delivery Times

All orders are processed within 5-7 days by our Print on Demand printing partner. Standard shipping typically takes between 1-2 business days for major cities and 2-3 business days for outlying areas.
If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Additionally, nationwide shipping delays (such as those caused by holiday volumes, strikes/unrest or inclement weather)
may impact your estimated delivery date.
